An authentic American Ice cream Parlour on Stag Hill

After listening to all students’ wishes, an American ice cream parlour serving 20 flavours will be on site during the summer.

Located at the hub of our Stag Hill campus and just by the lake, Angel’s Delights the ice cream parlour will now be opened on 5 July 2021, contributing different “flavours” to the American culture.

This ice cream parlour will be a place where it sells ice cream, milkshakes and sorbet from Monday to Saturday.

They include cookies & cream, mint-chocolate, sea salted caramel and more, available to purchase by the scoop and in a recyclable cup!

We make sure everyone can have a taste of the ice cream on campus, so there will be vegan and gluten free options available, with refreshing sorbet on offer.

20 flavours available!
Please keep 2 metres apart in the queue.
Vegan and Gluten Free options are available.

Nomm! All of the ice cream and sorbets are made locally with fresh ingredients.

Lovers can also add a range of delicious toppings to their ice cream cones including sprinkles and chocolate biscuits.

We have also made certain that the parlour is completely Covid-19 safe and socially distanced, with hand sanitiser available all the time.

It is hoped that the ice cream parlour could become a permanent fixture in the future.
